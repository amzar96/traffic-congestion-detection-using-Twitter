iTraffic - About Us

What is iTraffic?

Malaysia is one of the country that always having a traffic jammed problem especially during the peak hour and this problem causing some people either need to plan early move from their home or else they will be late to their desired locations. People always spend more than 53 minutes just for stuck the traffic jammed in Kuala Lumpur.

During that time, people always like to play smartphone and social media. Therefore, iTraffic takes this opportunity to analysis the tweet about traffic jammed and give user the information about the location that having traffic jammed. In this project, machine learning have been use to learn the tweet and predict the new tweet as a real-time.



The objective of this project is,
a To identify the causes of traffic congestion and make it as a keyword to scrape from the Twitter
b To analyse tweets and decide which area of Klang Valley having traffic congestion
c To develop the system that will give information to the user about traffic jam in certain location in Klang Valley
The scope of this project is,
a This research is only collected tweet in Malay language only.
b This research is focusing on traffic congestion in Klang Valley
c The system gives information according to the tweet that related to traffic jammed in the location
